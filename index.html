Braking Distance Calculation

brakingDistance (m) = (initialSpeed (m/s) ** 2) / (2 * brakingForce (N));

Cornering Force Calculation

corneringForce = (weight * (initialSpeedMps ** 2)) / (corneringRadius * tireCoefficient)
